South Africa continues to reign supreme in world rugby, maintaining their number one position in the World Rugby rankings with an impressive rating of 94.67 points.

The Springboks have been in outstanding form throughout 2026, defeating top-ranked nations including New Zealand, France, and Ireland in back-to-back test matches.

Head coach Rassie Erasmus praised his squad's dedication and discipline, stating that the team's focus remains on defending their World Cup title in Australia 2027.

Key players such as Siya Kolisi, Eben Etzebeth, and Cheslin Kolbe have been instrumental in the team's dominance, delivering world-class performances in every match.

With the Rugby World Cup just around the corner, South Africa looks like the team to beat, and rugby fans around the world are watching closely.
